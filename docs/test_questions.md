# Test Questions for SSA Data Assistant

Run these through the app. Give thumbs up to correct results to build the golden query library. Give thumbs down with feedback for incorrect ones so we can improve.

## Clients & Contacts
1. List all client firm names and their industries
2. What clients are in the Manufacturing industry?
3. Show me the contacts at AIG with their email addresses
4. Which clients have international geographic presence?
5. What resources know the client contacts at AIG?
6. Show all client contacts and which SSA resources are connected to them

## Consultants & Roles
7. Who are the Managing Directors?
8. List all consultants with their titles and phone numbers
9. Show me Directors and their email addresses
10. How many consultants do we have by title?

## ICs (Independent Contractors)
11. Who are our ICs and their SSA contacts?
12. List all independent contractors with their daily rates
13. Which SSA consultant is the contact for each IC?

## Tools & Capabilities
14. What tools does our firm use?
15. Show resources who use Power BI
16. Which resources have the Control Tower capability?
17. List all firm capabilities
18. What tools are associated with each capability?
19. Show resources who use Excel along with their role

## Engagements & Projects
20. List all active engagements with client names
21. What are the project names and their status?
22. Show engagements for AIG
23. Who is on the project team for each engagement?
24. What are the deliverables for our engagements?
25. Show engagements with their recurring annual impact

## Training & Courses
26. List all training courses
27. What capabilities are covered by each course?
28. Which courses teach Power BI?
29. Show courses with their tools and links

## Cross-domain
30. Which Managing Directors are connected to client contacts?
31. What tools do our Directors use?
32. Show ICs and the capabilities they have
33. List resources with both their tools and capabilities
34. What clients have engagements with case studies?

## Edge cases (test robustness)
35. How many clients do we have?
36. What is the average daily rate for ICs?
37. Show me everything about AIG
38. Who knows people at Maesa?
39. List people with the title Principal
40. What projects started in 2024?
